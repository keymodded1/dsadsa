Page 1: Daily Discoveries

Direwolves: Real or Fantasy?

Direwolves once roamed ancient North America. These giant wolves were larger and stronger than modern ones, capturing imaginations through shows like Game of Thrones. But make no mistake – they were real predators of the Ice Age!

Soccer Revolution by Kids

Children around the globe are inventing hybrid sports. One such game, “TagBall”, mixes soccer with tag, boosting fun and fitness. Schools love it for team building and creativity!

Ocean Cleanup Heroes

Young inventors are leading the way in ocean protection. From building trash-collecting boats to inventing eco-friendly nets, kids are saving marine life with technology and passion.

Page 2: World Wonders

Space Farming: Growing Beyond Earth

NASA is testing how plants grow in space. Kids can join experiments via virtual STEM camps, learning how future astronauts might eat fresh food on Mars!

Recycling Robots: Smart & Green

Engineers have built robots that sort trash automatically. A group of students in Germany even designed a robot that sings when it recycles correctly!

Forest Friends: Animal Communication

Did you know that ants “talk” using smells? Or that dolphins have names for each other? Scientists – and kids – are exploring how animals communicate in wild ways!
